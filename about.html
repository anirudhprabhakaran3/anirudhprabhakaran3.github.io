Hello! I'm a final year (senior) undergraduate at the National Institute of Technology Karnataka (NITK), Surathkal, India. I am interested in Computer Vision, Deep Learning, Medical Machine Learning and Theoretical Machine Learning.

Currently, I am working with Prof. Dianbo Liu of Cognitive AI for Science (CogAI4Sci) Lab, National University of Singapore (NUS). We are trying to implement GFlowOut on vision models for eye disease detection. We aim to improve the accuracy of these models, and increase interpretibility of the models.

Apart from machine learning, I am also experienced with web development. I have worked on projects in a variety of frameworks, including Flask and Django (Python), Rails and Jekyll (Ruby), and React and NextJS (JavaScript). Currently, I'm interested in functional languages, like Elixir and the Phoenix framework.

In my free time, I listen to music and read. Occasionaly, I write, sing and play the keyboard!
